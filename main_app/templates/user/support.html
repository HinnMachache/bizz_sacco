Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us.

What is SACCO?

SACCO stands for Savings and Credit Cooperative Organization, which is a member-based financial institution that provides savings, credit, and other financial services.

How can I apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, navigate to the loan application section on your dashboard and fill out the required details. You can also contact our support team for assistance.

What documents are required for loan applications?

Typically, you will need to provide proof of income, a valid ID, and any other documents required based on the type of loan you are applying for. Please check the specific requirements on the loan application page.

How can I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How can I contact support?

You can contact support by using the contact form provided on this page or by emailing us at support@example.com.
